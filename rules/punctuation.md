# Punctuation Rules

**Semicolons**: Only for complex list items. Join independent clauses with conjunctions instead (e.g., "the engine is pure while the store manages state", not "the engine is pure; the store manages state" or "X does this; Y handles that").

**Dashes**: ` – ` (space-en-dash-space) for asides and breaks. Em-dash `—` only in attribution or interrupted dialogue.
